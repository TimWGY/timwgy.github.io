A data enthusiast's urban dream

I am Tim Guangyu Wu, a recent graduate of MS in Data Science program at Columbia University. Currently, I am a research assistant at the Mapping Historical New York project. My research and work is at the intersection of data science and urban studies, with a focus on spatial history and social impact. I am interested in how urban morphology and mobility choices influence the development of different communities and how we can enhance equity and sustainability through data-driven design.
