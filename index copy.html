A data enthusiast's urban dream

Hi, I am Tim Wu, associate research scholar at the Center for Spatial Research of Columbia University. I received my Master's degree in Data Science from Data Science Institute at Columbia University and Bachelor's degree in Data Science and Finance from NYU Shanghai. At Center for Spatial Research, I lead the data science work on the Mapping Historical New York project, developing automated methods for map extraction and record linkage, as well as high spatial-temporal resolution data model for studying the urban history of NYC.

My broader research interest is to create healthier and greener cities through innovative application of data science in urban planning and policy.
